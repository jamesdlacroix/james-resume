Professional Profile

I’m a designer with over ten years experience running a small design and development studio. As an avid learner, I enjoy participating in all facets of the design process and continually explore new methods for delivering better user experiences.

Work Experience

November 2005–Present

Founded the studio to further explore the application of design thinking to a broader range of solutions and recipients.
